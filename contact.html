Marc Beiersdorf

I am a solo cinematographer and video editor with a deep passion for crafting stories that actually move people. Based in the beautiful southern part of Germany, my work is focused on premium event shootings, high-energy sports campaigns, fashion narratives, and cinematic shorts.

I’ve had the privilege of bringing visions to life for incredible partners like Adidas, Mercedes, Kärcher, and Scalable, alongside legendary teams like 1.FC Köln.

Whether we are building a brand narrative from the ground up or capturing the raw emotion of a live sports moment, I’m here to make it cinematic. If you are interested in collaborating, just send a message to the email below. I would love to hear from you. See you out there!
